Daily routine

  1. Wake up with gratitude
  2. Stretch and do 5-minute breathing
  3. Make a power smoothie
  4. Read one page from a motivational book
  5. Plan the day in a journal
  6. Study or attend classes
  7. Take a mindful walk outdoors
  8. Work on a personal project or hobby
  9. Connect with friends or family
  10. Watch a documentary or learn something new
  11. Reflect and unwind before sleep
